Refund Policy:

Triptoll, we are committed to provide greatest quality of service to our consumer. However, we understand that there may be some circumstance where refund is necessary. The following refund policy outline the conditions under the refund will be granted

Refund Eligibility:

You may raise the request for refund in the following circumstances

Booking cancellation: once you book the service and cancel the request of service within 6 hours, you are eligible to get 90% refund. If you request to cancel the service before process initiation to serve the request, you are eligible to get full refund

If you do not provide any confirmation within 6 hours, you are not eligible to raise the refund request.

Non-refundable situations:

  • Changes made to your booking after confirmation
  • If customer is fail to present during the scheduled day
  • Schedule the shipping time in unforeseen circumstances such as natural disaster and accident.

Refund Process:

To raise the request for refund

Contact Us: call to our office or connect through email and whats app on the provided number

Provide details: provide all the details including booking number, contact information and brief description with the genuine reason for the refund request

Processing time:

The refund request will be reviewed in maximum 5-7 business days. Once the review is done, refund will be processed back and will get the refund amount in another 3-4 business days.

Changes in the refund policy:

We have right to reserve to change in the refund policy in any time without any official notification. Any changes in the refund policy will be posted at the website refund policy section. It is advised to visit the official website to continue use of our service signifies acceptance in the updated policy.
